Hi, I'm Logan. By day I'm an IT Systems Engineer — by night I'm breaking into things in my homelab (legally).

I got into security through the infrastructure side, and the more I learned about how systems are built, the more I wanted to understand how they break. HackTheBox became my training ground, and this site is where I document what I learn along the way.

## What You'll Find Here

- **HackTheBox Writeups** — Detailed walkthroughs of retired machines, covering enumeration, exploitation, and privilege escalation
- **Security Research** — Notes on offensive security tools, Active Directory attacks, and lessons learned
- **Homelab** — Build logs, infrastructure projects, and things I'm tinkering with (coming soon)
- **Videos** — Walkthroughs and homelab content (coming soon)

## The Setup

All boxes are worked from a Kali VM running on Proxmox in my homelab. Notes are taken in real-time during the engagement, then polished into the writeups you see here.
