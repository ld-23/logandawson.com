Hi, I'm Logan. I work in security and enjoy breaking into things (legally).

This site is where I document my HackTheBox writeups and security research. Each writeup walks through the full attack chain — from initial recon to root — explaining the reasoning behind every step.

## What You'll Find Here

- **HackTheBox Writeups** — Detailed walkthroughs of retired machines, covering enumeration, exploitation, and privilege escalation
- **Tools & Techniques** — Notes on offensive security tools, Active Directory attacks, and lessons learned

## The Setup

All boxes are worked from a Kali VM in my homelab. Notes are taken in real-time during the engagement, then polished into the writeups you see here.
